The Best Movies Ever according to Jonah

My 10 favorite movies.

Dead Poets Society

The sad story of a teacher trying to bring joy to his students.

The Menu

Physcological Thriller meets comedy in a movie that is just the right amount of pretentious.

Alien

Perfect blend of Scifi and Horror.

Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid

A Western about outlaws on the run.

The Shining

The horror genre at its finest.

Inglorious Basterds

Fun and tense Nazi killing movie with just the right amount of Tarantino flair.

Beverly Hills Cop

Best Eddie Murphy comedy movie.

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

The start of the epic saga of The Lord of the Rings.

Good Will Hunting

A gifted individual gets help from a psychologist to find direction in life.

The Martian

Fun Scifi movie about surviving alone on Mars.
